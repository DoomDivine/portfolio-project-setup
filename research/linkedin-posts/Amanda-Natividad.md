Source

Summary

Amanda shares her unconventional path into marketing, from journalism and culinary training to leading marketing at SparkToro. She explains that the best marketing appears where audiences already spend their attention and argues that brand building and trust matter more than perfect attribution models. She also reinforces the idea of creating platform-native content rather than forcing users to leave the platforms they use.

Key Takeaway

The most effective marketing creates value where audiences already spend time rather than constantly pushing for clicks.

LinkedIn Post 3

Title: Everyone says "create valuable content"

Source

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_everyone-says-create-valuable-content-activity-7317562305267806210-Wi8C?utm



Summary

Amanda challenges the common belief that value is subjective. She argues that truly valuable content should either make someone smarter, help them improve, or create an emotional response. She encourages marketers to focus on utility rather than chasing engagement metrics alone.

Key Takeaway

Content should deliver measurable utility to the audience instead of simply attracting attention.
