B2B Content Marketing & Distribution

Source

Exit Five Conference Session

Summary

Ross explains his "Create Once, Distribute Forever" philosophy. He discusses how marketers can increase ROI by turning one core content asset into multiple formats and distributing it across LinkedIn, Reddit, newsletters, podcasts, and niche communities. He emphasizes that distribution is often a greater competitive advantage than content creation itself.

Key Takeaways

Distribution is often more important than creation.
One asset can be repurposed into many formats.
LinkedIn and Reddit are powerful distribution channels for B2B SaaS.
Great content without promotion has limited business impact.
Content systems improve scalability and consistency.

Relevance To LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy

Ross's work directly supports LinkedIn growth because he focuses on repurposing, distribution, audience engagement, and maximizing the reach of content assets across platforms.
