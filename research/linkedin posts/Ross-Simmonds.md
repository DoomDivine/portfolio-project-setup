Ross Simmonds

Why Selected

Ross Simmonds is the founder of Foundation Marketing and author of Create Once, Distribute Forever. He is widely recognized for his expertise in content distribution, B2B SaaS marketing, LinkedIn growth, and content repurposing. His work focuses on helping brands maximize the reach of their content through strategic distribution rather than simply creating more content.

LinkedIn Post 1

Title

How to Make Your Content Drive Business That Converts

Source:

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_how-to-make-your-content-drive-business-that-activity-7320060574183079936-82I6

Summary

Ross argues that many marketers mistakenly believe they have a content problem when they actually have a distribution problem. Instead of constantly producing new material, companies should maximize the value of existing assets by repurposing them into social posts, carousels, and community discussions.

Key Takeaway

One high-quality piece of content can generate significantly more impact when distributed effectively across multiple channels.

LinkedIn Post 2

Title

How to Turn Social Media Into Your Most Profitable Channel

Source:

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_how-to-turn-social-media-into-your-most-profitable-activity-7417585120473145346-OF92

Summary

Ross outlines a framework based on content pillars and the 80/15/5 rule: 80% value-driven content, 15% human-focused content, and 5% promotional content. He emphasizes that structured content systems outperform random posting habits.

Key Takeaway

Consistent content frameworks create more sustainable growth than posting without a clear strategy.

LinkedIn Post 3

Title

How To Do Content Marketing Effectively

Source:

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/rosssimmonds_marketing-activity-7241087116791668738-DzdT

Summary

Ross explains that content marketing consists of both content creation and content promotion. He argues that many businesses invest heavily in producing content but fail to actively market and distribute it after publication.

Key Takeaway

Creating great content is only half the job; successful marketers actively distribute and amplify their content.
