Summary

In this interview, Austin discusses how he built an audience of more than one million LinkedIn followers. He explains the importance of content systems, consistency, audience understanding, and creating value before expecting results. He also discusses the role of Twitter/X and newsletters in expanding audience reach beyond LinkedIn.

Key Takeaways

Content systems outperform random posting.
Audience trust compounds over time.
Consistency is a competitive advantage.
Multi-platform distribution increases reach.
Valuable content attracts opportunities and relationships.
