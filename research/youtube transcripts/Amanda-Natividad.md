Title

How to Win with Zero Click Content

Source

https://youtu.be/t6Yxdw-Sid4?si=5ytGppR-X2cPWipB



Summary

Amanda explains the principles behind Zero-Click Marketing, where marketers create valuable content directly on platforms such as LinkedIn, Reddit, and social media rather than focusing exclusively on driving website traffic. She discusses why many traditional attribution models fail to capture the real impact of content and explains how native content can drive awareness, trust, engagement, and eventual purchasing decisions. She also discusses content quality, audience research, and how modern marketers should think about visibility in an AI-driven environment.

Key Takeaways
Not all valuable content needs to generate clicks.
Native platform content often performs better than link-heavy content.
Brand awareness and trust are difficult to measure but highly valuable.
Audience research should guide content creation.
Modern marketing requires balancing visibility, engagement, and long-term brand building.
