LinkedIn Post 1

Title

Followers Didn't Bring Me More Revenue

Source

https://www.linkedin.com/posts/tasbober_more-followers-didnt-bring-me-more-revenue-activity-7296875977282924544-B85i

Summary

Tas challenges the common obsession with follower counts and viral posts. She argues that revenue comes from meaningful conversations with ideal customers rather than vanity metrics. She contrasts creators with large audiences but few sales against founders with small networks who consistently book qualified calls through targeted outreach and relevant content.

Key Takeaway

Followers and engagement are useful, but business results come from building relationships with the right audience.
