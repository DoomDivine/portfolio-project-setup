Title

Beehiiv CEO Tyler Denk – Why Email Beats Social Media

Video Link

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f0jeD24nmFw

Summary

Tyler discusses why email remains one of the most powerful audience-building channels despite the rise of social media. Drawing on his experience at Morning Brew and beehiiv, he explains the value of audience ownership, direct communication, and building sustainable creator businesses. He contrasts owned channels like email with algorithm-dependent social platforms and discusses long-term audience growth strategies.

Key Takeaways

Email provides direct access to your audience.
Audience ownership is more valuable than platform reach.
Building in public creates trust and transparency.
Consistent content compounds over time.
Newsletter businesses can become powerful growth engines.

Relevance To LinkedIn Organic Content Strategy

Tyler's content reinforces a key lesson from many of the other experts: LinkedIn should be used as a distribution and relationship-building channel, while creators should ultimately aim to own their audience through assets such as newsletters and email lists. This complements the audience-building principles discussed by Justin Welsh, Amanda Natividad, and Matt Gray.
